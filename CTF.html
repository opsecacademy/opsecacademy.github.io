🔑 How the Key System Works

Each team gets two codes at the start of term:

🟢 Public Key (npub) — your team's name badge. Share freely.
🔴 Private Key (nsec) — your team's FLAG. Split into 4 pieces and hidden in Minecraft.

If another team finds all 4 pieces and proves it with a signed Nostr message — they score big. Hide them well. 🏆

🟢 npub = Public Key → Share freely — your team's ID 🔴 nsec = Private Key → THIS IS YOUR FLAG — hide it in Minecraft! 💡 Padlock (public) vs. key that opens it (private) Only the key holder can prove who they are!
Setting Up nos2x (Chrome Extension) ▾

nos2x stores your Nostr private key in Chrome and signs messages on your behalf. A signed event is mathematical proof of flag capture.

1

Install nos2x

Search for nos2x in the Chrome Web Store. Click Add to Chrome.

Chrome Web Store → nos2x
2

Generate your team key pair

Visit the key generator. Copy both keys and save in your team folder. Teacher keeps a sealed copy.

nostrtool.com → Generate Key Pair npub1... ← PUBLIC KEY — share with class nsec1... ← PRIVATE KEY — THIS IS YOUR FLAG
3

Load your private key

Click the nos2x icon → Options → paste your nsec1... → Save.

4

Test it

Go to nostter.app → Login with extension. If nos2x asks to approve, it's working.

nostter.app → Login → Use Extension
5

Proving flag capture

Load the captured nsec into nos2x, sign a message with both team names, show the teacher. That signature is cryptographic proof.

After proving capture, reload your own nsec immediately. Write the captured key on paper first!
How Public & Private Keys Work ▾

The public key (npub) is your team's identity — share it everywhere. The private key (nsec) is your flag — keep it secret.

It is mathematically impossible to fake a signature without the private key. When a team signs a message using your nsec, the maths proves they found it. No screenshots needed. This is the same system that protects real identities on the Nostr network.

# PUBLIC KEY (npub) — share with everyone npub1abc123... ← your team's identity on Nostr # PRIVATE KEY (nsec) — THIS IS YOUR FLAG nsec1xyz789... ← split into 4 fragments, hide in Minecraft! # Capture flow: Enemy finds all 4 fragments → reconstructs nsec → loads into nos2x → signs a message → teacher verifies against known npub = CAPTURED!
