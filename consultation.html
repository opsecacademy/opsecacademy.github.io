Security Audit

Overview

This security audit is designed to enhance individual digital safety by assessing critical areas of online security. The audit generates a comprehensive report that includes personalised recommendations for protecting personal digital assets.

Key Areas Covered

Account Security
- Evaluation: Review of online accounts, emphasising strong password practices, password management tools, and two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Recommendations: Guidance on adjusting account settings and monitoring for unauthorised access.

System and Application Security
- Evaluation: Assessment of the importance of keeping software and applications updated to mitigate vulnerabilities.
- Recommendations: Specific actions to ensure all systems are current and secure.

Network Security
- Evaluation: Analysis of home Wi-Fi networks and internet connection safety.
- Recommendations: Steps to secure networks and enhance protection against external threats.

Data Backup
- Evaluation: Review of current data backup practices.
- Recommendations: Best practices for regular and secure backups to safeguard personal data.

Privacy Management
- Evaluation: Auditing of privacy settings and app permissions.
- Recommendations: Strategies to enhance control over personal information and maintain privacy.

Secure Browsing Practices
- Evaluation: Assessment of current online behaviours.
- Recommendations: Guidance on adopting safe online practices and using secure browsing tools.

Physical Security
- Evaluation: Review of hardware choices and physical security measures.
- Recommendations: Suggestions for improving overall physical security.

Advanced Security Measures
- Evaluation: Configuration and operation of secure systems, including the use of secure operating systems and tools for anonymous communication.
- Recommendations: Implementation of VPNs for protecting online activity and strategies for secure data management.

---

This security audit empowers individuals by providing a detailed report with actionable recommendations to enhance their digital security effectively.
