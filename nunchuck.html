Why Keys-Only Setup?

üéØ

Simplified Custody

By managing only the keys without the descriptor file, you reduce the attack surface and maintain clearer custody boundaries. The descriptor file is managed by your setup provider, while you maintain full control over transaction authorization.

üîí

Enhanced Separation

This model creates a clean separation between key custody (your responsibility) and wallet configuration (provider's responsibility), reducing the risk of configuration errors while maintaining your signing authority.

‚öñÔ∏è

Institutional Model

Many institutional custody solutions operate on this model, where key holders authorize transactions without direct access to the complete wallet descriptor, providing security through specialization.
