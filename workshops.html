TailsOS

A TailsOS workshop provides participants with a condensed yet comprehensive understanding of the operating system's installation, usage, and privacy features. Covering topics from basic navigation to advanced security measures, attendees learn to safeguard their digital activities through encrypted communication tools, safe browsing practices, and anonymity-enhancing features like the Tor browser. Hands-on activities and Q&A sessions ensure participants leave equipped with the knowledge and skills to leverage TailsOS effectively for enhanced digital privacy and security.

Password Management

The OpSec Academy's Password Management workshop provides participants with essential skills in safeguarding their digital identities through the use of Bitwarden and KeePassXC. In this comprehensive session, attendees learn the importance of robust password management practices in maintaining online security. Through hands-on training, they delve into the functionalities of Bitwarden and KeePassXC, mastering the art of generating strong, unique passwords and securely storing them in encrypted vaults. By embracing these industry-leading password management tools, participants empower themselves to navigate the digital realm with confidence, ensuring their sensitive information remains shielded from potential threats.
